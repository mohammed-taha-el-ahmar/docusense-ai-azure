MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement is made effective as of July 1, 2024, between ACME Corp ("Customer") and Vandelay Consulting ("Provider"). Provider shall provide professional services under one or more Statements of Work. Payment terms are net thirty (30) days from a correct invoice. Either party may terminate for convenience upon thirty (30) days' written notice. Aggregate liability is capped at fees paid in the preceding twelve months.
